• Quality Policy

Quality is important to our business because we value our customers and their repeat business. We strive to provide our customers with products and services which meet or exceed their expectations and lead to a long successful relationship between us. We pride ourselves in having a lasting relationship with our customers and being a one-stop shop for all their brass component needs.

Our strict adherence to high quality standards has gotten us where we are. We started off as a family company 33 years ago and have today evolved into one of the largest exporters of brass components from India.

This policy is posted on the Company Office and all employees are expected to strive every day in their work to live up to it.
